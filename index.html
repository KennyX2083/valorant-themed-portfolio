About Me

Hi, I'm Jerry. I'm a Computer Science student at Columbia University with a minor in Economics. Growing up, I worked in many different Chinese owned restaurants, both my family's restaurant and also family friends. I helped with packing orders, setting up tables, taking calls, and other necessary tasks. It was a fast-paced and hands-on environment that taught me responsibility and how to stay calm under pressure, while working in different restaurants often made me learn how to adapt to new environments.
I bring these skills into my work today. Whether I'm managing my time for my coursework, debugging my code, or trying to design a new feature, I always stay calm and try to bring myself to work for the best result possible. In my free time, I enjoy games of any kind, volleyball, star/moon-gazing (not very easy to do in NYC), and trying to think of fun ideas to turn into reality.
